Remember those GPS mapping cars that roamed the earth, with all of the cameras and fancy technology for data collection? At Nokia/Navteq, we had a fleet of vehicles and an army of engineers all over the world that needed to know how to use the latest tools available to them. So rather than fly them around for training ($$$), we designed and delivered remote learning experiences that could be completed from the road, wherever they were.
