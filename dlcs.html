Digital Leadership Challenge Simulation

User Experience Design / Apr 2013 - Nov 2014
 

The Brief

Simply put, human trafficking is modern-day slaver*y. Every year, men, wom*en, and children are trafficked in every part of the world, including here in the United States. Congress has tasked federal agencies, like the Department of Homeland Security, to work with private-sector partnerships to end human trafficking. As a part of this effort, our team collaborated with DHS' Blue Campaign to build a highly-interactive digital awareness and education campaign for DHS employees and members of the public.

My Role

Lead Designer + Project Manager
As lead designer on the project, I worked to ensure that the program was meeting the Blue Campaign's stated objectives. In this role, I met weekly with our Blue Campaign clients, pitching design concepts, providing status updates, and answering any technical questions that arose. Internally, I managed the 4-person design/production team on a daily basis, collaborating with visual designers, video editors, and instructional designers.
