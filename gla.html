GovLoop Academy & Learning Platform

Software Engineering & UX Design / Nov 2014 - Oct 2017
 

The Brief

As the saying goes, "Everyone hates their LMS" (Learning Management System). We needed to deliver and track hundreds of microlearning experiences for GovLoop's thousands of users. So rather than licensing an LMS we would grow to hate, we built our own. Powered by WordPress and LearnDash to track learning experiences via xAPI, GovLoop Academy was launched in April 2014 with 25 courses available for free to the GovLoop community.

Objective

Design and build a lightweight Learning Management System (LMS) to deliver and track hundreds of microlearning experiences for GovLoop's thousands of users and government clients alike.
